What is MemeBot2021?

MemeBot2021 is a project created for all those loving memes. Our group created it for study purposes for Web Developement classes. It's a chatbot that's supposed to help with finding your favorite memes by providing theirs description. MemeBot then will search through the web and respond to you with that meme.

Ok, but why?

Because we love memes and chatbots, so why not join those two together, and mix the practical part of it with pleasure.
