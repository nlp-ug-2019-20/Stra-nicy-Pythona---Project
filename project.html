About the project

What is MemeBot2021?



MemeBot2021 is a chatbot created for everyone who loves meme and everyone who struggles with keeping up with latest trends in meme community.



And how does it work?

MemeBot2021 uses 'Meme API' that's connected with Reddit's most popular Meme subreddits, so you’re in good hands! Just have a chat with MemeBot and he'll show you 'da wae' to random memes from across Reddit, and then sit back and relax, because MemeBot2021 is here for you to have a good time!



Also, one more thing...


Why is everything so green on this website?!

Color green is associated with harmony, freshness, safety, and peace, so no wonder we incorporated it into our chatbot!

We're hoping you'll feel a bit relaxed :)
